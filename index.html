NeuroDecode SSVEP

SSVEP EEG decoding from frequency-based representations to reproducible model comparison

Research Question

Can SSVEP EEG signals be transformed into frequency-based representations that reliably decode intended visual targets, and does performance improve when the model is matched to SSVEP physiology?

I started from a private OpenBCI/OpenVEP pilot recording, then used the public Wang2016 / Tsinghua SSVEP benchmark for the reproducible results. The private recordings are not included in this repository.

Main Result

At 1.2 seconds, parieto-occipital CCA decoded 134 out of 144 selected Wang2016 trials correctly, or 93.1% accuracy. At 4.0 seconds, cross-validated TRCA decoded 144 out of 144 trials correctly. The result supports the idea that SSVEP decoding improves when the representation and model are matched to frequency-locked visual cortex responses.
